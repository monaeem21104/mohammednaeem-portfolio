Healthcare Portal

HIPAA-compliant patient management system with appointment scheduling and telemedicine

This secure healthcare platform revolutionizes patient management by combining electronic health records, appointment scheduling, and telemedicine capabilities in one HIPAA-compliant solution.

Angular Django PostgreSQL WebRTC HIPAA Compliance OAuth 2.0

Project Highlights

Technical Implementation

Built with Angular for the responsive frontend and Django for the secure backend. Used PostgreSQL for reliable data storage and WebRTC for real-time telemedicine capabilities. Implemented full HIPAA compliance for patient data protection.

Challenges Overcome

Solved complex HIPAA compliance requirements with encryption at rest and in transit. Optimized video streaming for low-bandwidth scenarios. Created intuitive scheduling system that handles provider availability and patient preferences.

Client Satisfaction

Delivered on time despite strict regulatory requirements. Client reported 60% reduction in administrative workload and 35% increase in patient satisfaction. System became essential infrastructure for their medical practice.
